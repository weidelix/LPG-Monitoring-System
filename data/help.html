The following instructions are for assisting and guiding users on the proper using the LPG Monitoring Device.

Safety Instructions

  1. Choose a well-ventilated area for the device and tank placement. It should be away from any source of ignition and can be sheltered from the rain to avoid damages to the device.
  2. Turn off the device before charging the system because charging the system while using it can potentially result in slower performance, increased heat generation, reduced battery life, and risks associated with an unstable power supply.
  3. Do not wash the device directly with water instead, clean the chassis with a dry cloth.
  4. Please use at least 12 watts adapter to charge the device and remove it after 1hr - 2hrs of charging the battery.
  5. Always double check the wires and circuits of the device before turning it on.
  6. Put the power cord in the right place in order to avoid any stumble.
  7. Changes made to the system by unauthorized individuals or other technical modifications are strictly prohibited.

Parts of the device

  • Base Chasis
  • 3D Printed Chasis for the Electronics
  • Surface Platform for the Sytem
  • Micro-USB Charger

Installation

  1. Pull out the 3D printed chassis inside the system. Open the 3D printed Chassis. Before anything else, make sure that all hardware components are connected in the PCB. Once done.
  2. Insert your sim card into the GSM module which is also located into the chassis.
  3. Put the 18650 Rechargeable Lithium Batteries on the Battery Shield V8. Be aware of the positive and negative sides of the battery.
  4. Turn on the device by pressing the power button inside.
  5. Wait for the device to boot-up.
  6. After making sure that the components are turned on. Close the 3D printed chassis and push it inside the system.
  7. Place the system under or close to the cooking area, wherein LPG cylinders are present.

User Interface

  1. Set the notification warning level. When the LPG reaches the defined level, it will send you a notication.
  2. Select the tank type or customize the tare weight and the weight if needed.
  3. Add recipients for the SMS Notification then save.

Maintenance

  1. Turn off the device inside the 3D Printed chassis.
  2. You must use dry cloth to wipe the dust from the platform
  3. Use small and soft brushes to clean the dust inside the device.
For the hardware maintenance;
  1. Pull the front of the system where the chassis is facing and check the wiring inside, and test all the sensors.
  2. To test the sensors, open the device during the testing and test the system by putting an object that has known weight and putting it on the surface platform of the system. Visit 192.168.4.1 to see if it represents the correct value.
For the software maintenance;
  1. Strictly, do not change any that are present in the source code.
  2. Call the service provider.
NOTE: ALWAYS ASK OR CALL THE SERVICE PROVIDER FOR ANY CONCERN ABOUT THE DEVICE. CAUTION: ALWAYS REMOVE THE BATTERY OF THE DEVICE BEFORE DOING MAINTENANCE.
