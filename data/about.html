ESP32-BASED LPG MONITORING SYSTEM WITH SMS NOTIFICATION is a reliable and user-friendly system for monitoring the LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) levels in a gas tank. The system combines hardware components including the ESP32 microcontroller, GSM module, and load cell to accurately measure and monitor the gas weight level.
